W1-AT from Geonworks

This keyboard is from south korean machinist and designer Geonworks. They make custom mechanical keyboards housings and PCBS for enthusiast. Geonworks has made many models since the first korean only F1-8X, including F2-84, Frog TKL, Frog mini, Glare TKL and the one shown in these photos the W1-AT which is a modern redesign of the old IBM Model F AT. This keyboard in particular is made with Gateron black ink linear switches, lubed with krytox 205g0 on the housing and stem and krytox 105 oil on the spring inside the switch, the switches has also been filmed with deskeys 0.3mm gasket films for improved sound and stability. I have recorded a 1 minute playback of a typing test if you want to check out how it sounds.
